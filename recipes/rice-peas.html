Rice and Peas

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Red Beans
  • Thyme
  • Coconut Milk
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Wash rice and beans.
  2. Add 1:1 ratio of rice and coconut milk to Instant Pot.
  3. Add crushed garlic and thyme.
  4. Cook on high for 3 minutes and natural release for 10 minutes.
